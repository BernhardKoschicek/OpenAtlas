Content
=======

.. toctree::

Displayed texts in available languages can be customized here:

* **Intro** - displayed at the top at the start page before logging in
* **Contact** - displayed at the contact site, e.g. the webmaster and project lead
* **Legal notice** - displayed at the legal notice site, e.g. information about the institute

Links to **Contact** and **Legal notice** are available in the menu if they contain text.
